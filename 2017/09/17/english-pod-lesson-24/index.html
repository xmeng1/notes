Eating and Restaurant

Dialogue Summary

Do you two have any plans for the evening?

We were thinking of checking out a restaurant in the neigbourhood. Do you have any suggestions?

I know this really nice Italian place. The food is fantastic, and the d´ecor is beautiful. I’d recommend giving it a try.

Actually, I’m not all that crazy about Italian food; I’m in the mood for something a bit lighter.

In that case, I know a great little bistro. They make a really tasty seafood platter; the ﬁsh is outstanding.

It sounds fantastic, but I’m allergic to seafood, so. . .

Okay, well, let me think. . . Oh, I know this great little place. It’s just a hole in the wall, but they do the most amazing sandwiches. You gotta give them a try.

Ella, you took me there last time I visited, and I got food poisoning, remember?

Vocabulary Preview

  1. Food poisoning: not good thing and when you eat bad food that make you sick.
  2. allergic something.

Language Takeaway

  1. Check out
  2. Bistral bistro: not so cheap and so expensive, French.
  3. Not all that crazy about.
  4. Tasty. It is tasty
  5. In the mode for: want to do

Fluency builder

  1. Do you have some suggestions/ideas?
  2. I’d recommend give it to try
  3. You’d got give it a try
